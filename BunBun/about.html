Bun Bun Bakery Shop

Since 1995

Bun Bun Bakery Shop is one of the oldest bakery shops in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. In 1995, Bun Bun Bakery opened its first location on a quiet street corner in the heart of Pittsburgh city near Carnegie Mellon University. From its inception, Bun Bun Bakery has been cherished for its classic American baked goods, vintage decor and warm, inviting atmosphere.
